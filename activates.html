What do children do in ABEG family?

What do children do in ABEG family? Countless of these children come to classes in different batches every hour/hour and a half. Apart from studying in the class, we regularly exchange ideas with them. They are completely free to express their opinion. There is no barrier for them to speak. Apart from dancing, singing, reciting, reading classes, they also enjoy fun classes every week. They work in a democratic system. They elect their own representatives. They have two councils of their own called Asharya Chhatra Parishad (ACHAP) and We Save Ourselves (WSO), which are very effective. Through this they try to eliminate all their small problems themselves.

Is AABEG an unpaid coaching center ??

Since we help children in education and we have children from nursery class to higher secondary class in that continuum, most of the people think of EMOTION as an unpaid coaching center. Children are also admitted first thinking about that. We make it clear to the children and their parents at the outset that Egho is not a school or a coaching center. Children's academic results are not our goal. If they get good results, if not, we have no obligation. We want them to be good people. We don't make our children dream.
